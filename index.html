The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or sell any collected data with third parties.

We retain the collected data for a period of 12 months. After this period, the data is securely deleted from our systems.

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information:

7. Cookies and Tracking

AppVajra does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.

8. Your Data Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there are no options for users to access, modify, or delete their data. The data we collect is not personally identifiable.

9. Age Restrictions
